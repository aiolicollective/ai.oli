Data Deletion Policy — n8n-social-aioli

Last updated: October 31, 2025

About

n8n-social-aioli is an internal workflow operated by the /ai.oli collective to automate social media content publication and interactions across multiple client accounts.

/ai.oli acts as a creative and automation service provider for multiple clients. The system does not collect, retain, or share any personal data. All data is processed in memory solely to complete automation tasks.

Data deletion

As no personal data is stored, no deletion process is required. All temporary data (e.g., post IDs, tokens, captions) is automatically discarded after the workflow execution.

Revoking access

If you have connected your Facebook, Instagram, or other social accounts via our automation system and wish to revoke access:

  1. Go to your account settings (e.g., Facebook → Settings → Apps and Websites)
  2. Find “n8n-social-aioli”
  3. Select “Remove” to revoke access permissions

Infrastructure

We use private infrastructure for internal automation and secure cloud services (e.g., Cloudinary for media storage). No personal data is stored on public servers or shared with any third parties.
